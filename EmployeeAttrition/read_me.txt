-It's Machine learning based project which checks whether and employee will leave his job based on certain data inputs.
- Decision is made based on classifications and then choosing most common result (Ensemble Learning) based on data in 
"/Platform/documents/Employee_Attrition - Employee_Attrition.csv"

- 2 Functions are present :
a) Train model and then give result
b) Give result based on already trained model by de- pickling the object , as training model consumes time.
